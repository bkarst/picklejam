Quick reference:
- **Global CSS:** link `styles.css` (imports all tokens + fonts). Namespace for components is `PickleJamDesignSystem_26eda6`.
- **Colors:** court green `#0F3D2E`, electric lime `#C6F045`, hot pink `#FF4FA3`, soft pink `#FFD6E6`, cream `#FFF7E6`.
- **Type:** Archivo Black (uppercase display, often skewed −6°), Montserrat (body/subhead).
- **Signature look:** chunky rounded pills + the "sticker" treatment (2px dark-green outline + hard offset shadow).
- **Components:** `components/` (forms, display, brand). **UI kit:** `ui_kits/platform/`. **Assets:** `assets/`.
