- **Cluster into pillar + supporting posts.** Each pillar term (e.g. "pickleball rules," "how to play pickleball," "pickleball court dimensions") anchors a hub page; the themed long-tails become supporting articles that internally link up. This topical-authority structure is how Low-competition informational content outranks incumbents.

Add a printable/interactive court-diagram page.
